Privacy Policy for Driver Tracker

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This policy explains what data Driver Tracker ("the App") collects, how it's used, and where it's stored. Your trip, financial, and profile records stay on your device - there is no account system and we never sell your data. The App does use Firebase (Google) for crash reporting and basic usage analytics, described below.

Data the App Collects

DataWhy it's collectedWhere it's stored
GPS locationTo track mileage during a shift and to detect individual trips automaticallyOn your device only

The App does not require an account and does not collect your name, email, or any other personal identifier as part of analytics or crash reporting. Your location, receipts, voice audio, and financial records are never included in analytics events or crash reports, and are never transmitted anywhere except as described in this policy.

How Data Is Used

  • Location is used exclusively to calculate mileage and detect trip start/end points while a shift is active. It is never transmitted to any server operated by us or any third party.
  • Receipt photos and voice audio are processed entirely on your device using Google's on-device ML Kit and speech recognition. Nothing is uploaded.
  • Trip, revenue, and expense records you enter are used to calculate totals, generate the tax estimates shown in the app, and produce CSV exports you initiate yourself.

Data Storage and Backup

All data is stored locally in the app's private storage on your device. If you have Android's automatic backup enabled (the default on most devices), this data is backed up to your own Google Drive account by Android itself - we do not operate or have access to that backup. Uninstalling the app or disabling backups will remove this data permanently unless you've exported it yourself (the app includes a CSV export feature for this purpose).

Permissions Requested

  • Location (including background) - for shift/mileage tracking.
  • Camera and Photos - for receipt scanning.
  • Microphone - for voice-entry logging.
  • Notifications - to show a persistent reminder while a shift is active.

Each permission is requested only when the corresponding feature is first used, and the app remains usable (with that feature disabled) if a permission is declined.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google's on-device ML Kit (text recognition) and Android's Speech-to-Text APIs, both of which run locally on your device. No data processed by these APIs is sent to Google or any other party by this app.

The App also uses these Firebase (Google) services:

  • Firebase Crashlytics - automatically collects crash reports and error diagnostics (e.g. device model, OS version, app version, and the stack trace of what failed) so we can identify and fix bugs. It does not receive your trips, financial records, location, receipts, or voice audio.
  • Firebase Analytics - automatically collects basic usage data (e.g. which screens you open, how often the app is opened) to help us understand which features are actually used. It does not receive your trips, financial records, location, receipts, or voice audio.

Both services are operated by Google and governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Data sent to these services may be used by Google in accordance with that policy and is retained according to Firebase's standard data-retention settings.
