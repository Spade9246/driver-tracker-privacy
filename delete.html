Delete My Account & Data — Driver's Log

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Driver's Log stores all your trip, financial, and profile data locally on your device. This page explains how to delete your data from within the app or by contacting us.

Option 1 — Delete data directly in the app (fastest)

Step 1. Open Driver's Log on your device.
Step 2. Tap the menu (⋮) on the Home screen → Settings.
Step 3. Scroll to Account & Data → tap Delete all data.
Step 4. Confirm. All trips, expenses, revenue entries, vehicles, and profile data stored on your device will be permanently removed.
Step 5. Uninstall the app to ensure no residual data remains.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Send a deletion request to: arthoriusnetwork@gmail.com

Include "Data Deletion Request — Driver's Log" in the subject line and the email address associated with your Google account (if you used sign-in). We will process your request within 30 days.
